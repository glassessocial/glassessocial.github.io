About the App

Sign Up

The user first has to sign up with a username, password, and a location which will be a general area determined by the app.

Update information

After signing up the user is then sent to a screen where the user can input their favorite music, movies, and hangout spots.

Connect with others

Now that the user has updated their information they are able to see the top hangout places, music, and movies from the area they are in which is determined by the app.

Take it with you

By using the users general area the app is able to tell the user the best hangout spots of the area as well as the general culture and things enjoyed in that area. This allows the user to better connect with his or her surroundings.
